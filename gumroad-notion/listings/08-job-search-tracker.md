### What's Inside:

- **Application Pipeline** — Visual kanban: Bookmarked → Applied → Phone Screen → Interview → Offer. See your entire pipeline at a glance.
- **Company Research** — Glassdoor ratings, culture notes, pros/cons, and interview process details for every target company
- **Contact & Networking Manager** — Track recruiters, referrals, and connections with follow-up reminders
- **Interview Prep** — Log every interview: questions asked, your answers, interviewer feedback, and performance rating
- **Skills Inventory** — Track your skills, identify gaps, and flag what's on your resume
- **Resources** — Job boards, resume tools, and prep materials
- **Dashboard** — Pipeline overview, key stats (total applied, interviews, offers), upcoming interviews, follow-ups due
