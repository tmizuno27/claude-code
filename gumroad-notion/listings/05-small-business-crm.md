### FAQ:

**How is this different from HubSpot/Salesforce?**
It's simpler, free to run, fully customizable, and lives in Notion where you already work.

**Can my team use it?**
Yes — share the Notion page with team members. Works great for teams up to 10.

**Does it integrate with email?**
Not automatically — you log interactions manually. This takes seconds and ensures nothing is missed.
