### Why Most Planners Fail for ADHD:

- **Too many tasks**: Decision paralysis. You stare at a list of 20 items and do none.
- **No energy awareness**: They schedule tasks without considering your energy fluctuates wildly.
- **Guilt-based design**: "You missed 5 habits!" makes you abandon the whole system.
- **No brain dump**: Your 47 random thoughts have nowhere to go, so they hijack your focus.
- **Ignore hyperfocus**: Your superpower gets treated as a bug.

**This template fixes all of that.**

### What Makes This Different:

- **Maximum 3 tasks per day.** Not 10. Not 20. Three. Your brain works better with fewer choices.
- **Energy-based scheduling.** Match tasks to your energy level. Low energy day? Low-energy tasks. No guilt.
- **Brain Dump Inbox.** Random thought? Dump it. Sort it later. Or never. The point is: it's out of your head.
- **Hyperfocus Logger.** Track when you hyperfocus, what triggers it, and whether it was productive. Turn your superpower into a strategy.
- **Dopamine Rewards.** Each task has a reward field. Finished the boring task? You earned that fancy coffee.
- **No-guilt design.** Missed a day? The system resets. No streaks lost, no shame spirals, no red warnings.
