# New Product Proposals — 2026-03-27

Based on market research: Gumroad trending categories, low-competition niches, and high-demand segments.

## Proposal 1: ADHD Daily Planner & Focus System (RECOMMENDED - BUILD FIRST)

**Why**: ADHD-friendly Notion templates have extremely low competition but very high search volume. Most existing templates are generic planners with an "ADHD" label slapped on. A genuinely ADHD-optimized system (body doubling timers, dopamine reward tracking, executive function support) would stand out.

- **Price**: $14
- **Target**: Adults with ADHD (diagnosed or self-identified) — estimated 10% of adults
- **Differentiator**: Not just a planner. Designed around ADHD brain: visual timers, dopamine tracking, energy-based task sorting, hyperfocus session logger, "brain dump" inbox, medication tracker
- **Competition**: ~15 ADHD Notion templates on Gumroad (vs 500+ generic planners)
- **Search Volume**: "ADHD planner notion" — high and growing
- **Estimated Demand**: HIGH
- **Risk**: LOW

## Proposal 2: Freelance Client Portal Template

**Why**: Freelancers want to look professional without paying for Dubsado/HoneyBook. A client-facing Notion portal (project status, deliverables, invoices, feedback) is a growing niche. Few good options exist.

- **Price**: $17
- **Target**: Freelance designers, developers, consultants
- **Differentiator**: Client-FACING template (not just internal tracking). Share with clients for real-time project visibility.
- **Competition**: Very low (~5 dedicated client portal templates)
- **Risk**: LOW

## Proposal 3: AI Business Automation Prompt Pack

**Why**: Market research shows "AI workflow templates" and "business automation prompts" are top-selling categories in 2026. Specific niche: prompts for automating repetitive business tasks with AI (email sequences, SOPs, hiring, onboarding, reporting).

- **Price**: $19
- **Target**: Solopreneurs, small business owners using AI daily
- **Differentiator**: 60+ prompts organized by business function (Operations, HR, Finance, Customer Service, Marketing). Not generic — each prompt includes context, variables, and expected output.
- **Competition**: Medium (many generic prompt packs, few business-function-specific)
- **Risk**: LOW

## Proposal 4: Knowledge Management System (Second Brain Pro)

**Why**: Market research shows knowledge management has only 787 sellers but 232 monthly searches — best supply/demand ratio. This would be a standalone, more advanced version of the knowledge base in Life OS.

- **Price**: $19
- **Target**: Researchers, consultants, writers, lifelong learners
- **Differentiator**: Full Zettelkasten + PARA + progressive summarization. Literature notes, permanent notes, MOCs (Maps of Content), project notes, daily notes. More depth than Life OS.
- **Competition**: LOW (787 sellers, most are basic)
- **Risk**: MEDIUM (overlaps slightly with Life OS)

## Proposal 5: Remote Team Meeting & Standup System

**Why**: Remote work templates for virtual meetings and team tasks are in high demand with few good Notion solutions. Post-COVID remote work is permanent.

- **Price**: $14
- **Target**: Remote team leads, project managers, distributed teams
- **Differentiator**: Meeting agendas, standup logs, async decision tracker, retrospectives, 1-on-1 templates, action item tracker — all linked.
- **Competition**: LOW
- **Risk**: MEDIUM (B2B audience harder to reach on Gumroad)

**Rationale**:
