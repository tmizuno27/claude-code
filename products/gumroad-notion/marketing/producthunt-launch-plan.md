## Launch Strategy

### Pre-Launch (7 days before)

1. **Build a "Coming Soon" page** on Product Hunt
2. **Collect upvotes** — share the upcoming page with:
   - X/Twitter followers (@prodhq27)
   - Reddit communities where we've posted (DM engaged commenters)
   - Any email list / Gumroad followers
3. **Prepare all assets:**
   - Hero image: 1270x760px — dark gradient, all 10 template names in grid, "$49" pricing, "66% OFF" badge
   - Gallery images (5): One screenshot per top template (Freelance OS, Life OS, CRM, Content Creator, Side Hustle)
   - GIF/video: 30-second walkthrough of the bundle
4. **Line up a Hunter** (optional but helpful):
   - Reach out to active Product Hunt users in the productivity/Notion space
   - Offer free bundle in exchange for hunting

### Launch Day

**Target Day:** Tuesday or Wednesday (highest Product Hunt traffic)

**Recommended Date:** First available Tuesday after preparation is complete

**Timeline (all times EST):**

- **12:01 AM:** Product goes live (PH launches at midnight Pacific)
- **6:00 AM:** First social media push
  - X post with Product Hunt link
  - Update Gumroad listing to mention "Featured on Product Hunt"
- **8:00 AM:** Engage with every comment on PH page within 15 minutes
- **10:00 AM:** Second X post with early traction screenshot
- **12:00 PM:** Reddit posts in r/Notion and r/SideProject with PH link
- **3:00 PM:** Third social push — share any positive comments/reviews
- **6:00 PM:** Thank-you post on X regardless of ranking
- **11:59 PM:** Final engagement round on PH comments

**Key Rules:**
- Respond to EVERY comment on the PH page
- Be genuine and helpful — answer questions in detail
- Don't ask for upvotes directly (against PH guidelines)
- Share the PH link, not the Gumroad link, on launch day (drives PH ranking)

### Post-Launch (7 days after)

1. **Share results** on X — "We launched on Product Hunt. Here's what happened."
2. **Update Gumroad listing** with "Featured on Product Hunt" badge if applicable
3. **Write a "lessons learned" Reddit post** in r/SideProject or r/Entrepreneur
4. **Retarget visitors** — add PH badge to all social bios temporarily
5. **Follow up** with every PH commenter who showed interest
