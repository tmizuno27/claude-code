Tab Manager & Session Saver - Smart Tab Productivity

Tired of tab overload? Tab Manager & Session Saver helps you take control of your browser tabs with powerful organization tools.

KEY FEATURES:

** Tab Overview **
- See all open tabs at a glance with titles, URLs, and favicons
- Instantly search and filter through your tabs
- Click any tab to switch to it
- Close tabs directly from the popup

** Session Management **
- Save your current tab set with a custom name
- Restore saved sessions with one click
- Never lose your research context again

** Duplicate Detection **
- Automatically highlights duplicate tabs
- One-click removal of all duplicates
- Keep your browser clean and fast

** Drag & Drop Organization **
- Reorder tabs by dragging within the popup
- Changes reflect in your actual browser tabs

** Dark Mode **
- Beautiful light and dark themes
- Matches your system preference

** Pro Features (Upgrade) **
- Unlimited session saves (Free: 3 sessions)
- Export sessions as JSON backup
- Import sessions from backup files

KEYBOARD SHORTCUT:
Press Ctrl+Shift+T (Cmd+Shift+T on Mac) to instantly open Tab Manager.

PRIVACY:
- No data collection whatsoever
- All data stored locally on your device
- No analytics, no tracking, no external servers
- Minimal permissions: only "tabs" and "storage"

Perfect for researchers, developers, students, and anyone who works with many tabs. Save hours every week with smarter tab management.
