Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Overview

Tab Manager & Session Saver ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information processed by the Extension remains entirely on your local device.

Data Storage

The Extension uses Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API to store your saved sessions and preferences. This data is stored locally in your browser profile and is never sent to any external server.

Permissions

  • tabs: Required to read tab titles, URLs, and favicons for display and session saving.
  • storage: Required to save your sessions and preferences locally.

No additional permissions (such as host permissions or access to web page content) are requested.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

The Extension does not share any data with third parties. Period.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected in the Extension's store listing and this document.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.
