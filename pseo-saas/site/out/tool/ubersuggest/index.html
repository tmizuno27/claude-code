About Ubersuggest

Ubersuggest is a ai seo & marketing platform founded in 2017. It positions itself as “Neil Patel's affordable SEO tool for keyword ideas and site audits” and is best suited for beginners, solopreneurs, small businesses. Pricing starts at $29/mo, and a free plan is available.
