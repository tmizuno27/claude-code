Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Adobe Express is best suited for Adobe users, social media creators, marketers, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Designs.ai, meanwhile, shines for small businesses, marketers, non designers, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2021, Adobe Express describes itself as "Adobe's quick design tool with Firefly AI generation for everyone." Designs.ai, established in 2019, positions itself as "All-in-one AI creative platform for logos, videos, mockups, and copy." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Adobe Express** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (7.0 vs 6.0), more features (9 vs 7), availability of a free plan. However, this does not mean Designs.ai is a poor choice — far from it. Designs.ai excels in its own right, particularly for small businesses and marketers. Our recommendation: if you value adobe firefly ai generation built in, go with Adobe Express. If multiple design tools in one platform matters more to you, Designs.ai is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
