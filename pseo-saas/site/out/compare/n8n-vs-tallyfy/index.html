Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. n8n is best suited for developers, self hosters, privacy conscious teams, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Tallyfy, meanwhile, shines for small businesses, process documentation, client workflows, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2019, n8n describes itself as "Fair-code workflow automation platform with self-hosting option." Tallyfy, established in 2015, positions itself as "Process and workflow management for documenting and tracking business procedures." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **n8n** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (8.0 vs 6.0), more features (10 vs 7), availability of a free plan. However, this does not mean Tallyfy is a poor choice — far from it. Tallyfy excels in its own right, particularly for small businesses and process documentation. Our recommendation: if you value self-hostable and fair-code licensed, go with n8n. If simple process documentation matters more to you, Tallyfy is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
