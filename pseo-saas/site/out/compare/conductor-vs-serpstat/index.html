Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Conductor is best suited for enterprise SEO teams, large publishers, marketing organizations, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Serpstat, meanwhile, shines for SMBs, in house SEOs, budget teams, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2010, Conductor describes itself as "Enterprise SEO and content marketing platform for organic growth." Serpstat, established in 2013, positions itself as "Growth hacking SEO tool for keyword research, rank tracking, and site audit."
