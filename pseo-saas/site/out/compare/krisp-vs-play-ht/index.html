Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Krisp is best suited for remote workers on calls, noisy environment meetings, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Play.ht, meanwhile, shines for developers needing TTS API, podcast intros and voiceovers, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2017, Krisp describes itself as "AI noise cancellation for calls and meetings." Play.ht, established in 2016, positions itself as "Ultra-realistic AI voice generator and text-to-speech." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Krisp** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (8.0 vs 7.0), more competitive pricing, availability of a free plan. However, this does not mean Play.ht is a poor choice — far from it. Play.ht excels in its own right, particularly for developers needing TTS API and podcast intros and voiceovers. Our recommendation: if you value best-in-class noise cancellation, go with Krisp. If high-quality voices matters more to you, Play.ht is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
