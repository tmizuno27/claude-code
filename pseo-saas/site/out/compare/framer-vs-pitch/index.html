Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Framer is best suited for designers, startups, portfolio sites, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Pitch, meanwhile, shines for startups, remote teams, collaborative decks, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2013, Framer describes itself as "AI-powered website builder with professional design and CMS capabilities." Pitch, established in 2018, positions itself as "Collaborative presentation platform for modern teams with AI assistance." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Framer** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (8.0 vs 7.0), availability of a free plan. However, this does not mean Pitch is a poor choice — far from it. Pitch excels in its own right, particularly for startups and remote teams. Our recommendation: if you value beautiful design-first website builder, go with Framer. If excellent real-time collaboration matters more to you, Pitch is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
