Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. QuillBot is best suited for paraphrasing, summarizing, academic writing, students, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. SEO.ai, meanwhile, shines for SEO optimized content, content scoring, keyword targeting, SEO teams, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2017, QuillBot describes itself as "AI paraphrasing and summarization tool trusted by students." SEO.ai, established in 2022, positions itself as "AI-first SEO writing tool with real-time optimization scoring." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **SEO.ai** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to more features (7 vs 5), availability of a free plan. However, this does not mean QuillBot is a poor choice — far from it. QuillBot excels in its own right, particularly for paraphrasing and summarizing. Our recommendation: if you value real-time seo scoring as you write, go with SEO.ai. If best paraphrasing tool with multiple modes matters more to you, QuillBot is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
