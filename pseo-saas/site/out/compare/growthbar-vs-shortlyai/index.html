Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. GrowthBar is best suited for SEO blog posts, keyword research, content outlines, small teams, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. ShortlyAI, meanwhile, shines for blog posts, long form articles, stories, distraction free writing, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2020, GrowthBar describes itself as "SEO-focused AI writing tool with keyword research built in." ShortlyAI, established in 2020, positions itself as "Distraction-free AI writing tool focused on long-form content." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.
