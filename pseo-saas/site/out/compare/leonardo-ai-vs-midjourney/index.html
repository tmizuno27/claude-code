Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Leonardo AI is best suited for game assets, character design, concept art, custom styles, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Midjourney, meanwhile, shines for digital art, illustrations, concept art, creative projects, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2022, Leonardo AI describes itself as "AI image platform with custom model training and game assets." Midjourney, established in 2022, positions itself as "The leading AI image generator known for stunning artistic quality." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Midjourney** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (9.0 vs 8.0). However, this does not mean Leonardo AI is a poor choice — far from it. Leonardo AI excels in its own right, particularly for game assets and character design. Our recommendation: if you value best-in-class artistic quality and aesthetics, go with Midjourney. If generous free tier with 150 daily tokens matters more to you, Leonardo AI is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
