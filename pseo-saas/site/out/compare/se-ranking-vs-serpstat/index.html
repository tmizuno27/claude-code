Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. SE Ranking is best suited for small agencies, freelancers, SMBs, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Serpstat, meanwhile, shines for SMBs, in house SEOs, budget teams, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2013, SE Ranking describes itself as "Affordable all-in-one SEO platform with accurate rank tracking." Serpstat, established in 2013, positions itself as "Growth hacking SEO tool for keyword research, rank tracking, and site audit." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **SE Ranking** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (8.0 vs 7.0), more features (10 vs 9), more competitive pricing. However, this does not mean Serpstat is a poor choice — far from it. Serpstat excels in its own right, particularly for SMBs and in house SEOs. Our recommendation: if you value excellent value for an all-in-one tool, go with SE Ranking. If good value for comprehensive features matters more to you, Serpstat is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
