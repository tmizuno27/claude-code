Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. AI Writer is best suited for research articles, cited content, SEO writing, fact based content, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Longshot AI, meanwhile, shines for fact checked content, SEO articles, blog posts, research heavy writing, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2015, AI Writer describes itself as "AI article writer that cites sources and verifies claims." Longshot AI, established in 2021, positions itself as "AI writing tool with built-in fact-checking and citations." With 6 years between them, AI Writer brings the maturity and proven track record of a veteran platform, while Longshot AI offers the fresh perspective and modern architecture of a newer entrant.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Longshot AI** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (6.0 vs 5.0), more features (7 vs 3), more competitive pricing, availability of a free plan. However, this does not mean AI Writer is a poor choice — far from it. AI Writer excels in its own right, particularly for research articles and cited content. Our recommendation: if you value unique fact-checking and citation features, go with Longshot AI. If auto-generates citations and sources matters more to you, AI Writer is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
