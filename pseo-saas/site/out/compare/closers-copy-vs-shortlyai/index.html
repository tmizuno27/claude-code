Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Closers Copy is best suited for sales copy, landing pages, email sequences, conversion copywriting, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. ShortlyAI, meanwhile, shines for blog posts, long form articles, stories, distraction free writing, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2020, Closers Copy describes itself as "AI copywriting tool built on proven sales frameworks." ShortlyAI, established in 2020, positions itself as "Distraction-free AI writing tool focused on long-form content." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Closers Copy** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (6.0 vs 5.0), more features (7 vs 2), more competitive pricing. However, this does not mean ShortlyAI is a poor choice — far from it. ShortlyAI excels in its own right, particularly for blog posts and long form articles. Our recommendation: if you value built on proven copywriting frameworks (aida, pas, etc.), go with Closers Copy. If clean distraction-free interface matters more to you, ShortlyAI is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
