Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Airtable AI is best suited for operations teams, content teams, project managers, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Hive, meanwhile, shines for marketing teams, mid size companies, collaborative teams, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2012, Airtable AI describes itself as "AI-enhanced flexible database platform for organizing any type of work." Hive, established in 2015, positions itself as "AI-enhanced project management platform with flexible views and team tools." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Airtable AI** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (8.0 vs 7.0), availability of a free plan. However, this does not mean Hive is a poor choice — far from it. Hive excels in its own right, particularly for marketing teams and mid size companies. Our recommendation: if you value most flexible database-spreadsheet hybrid, go with Airtable AI. If flexible project views matters more to you, Hive is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
