Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Aider is best suited for terminal first developers, open source enthusiasts, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. JetBrains AI, meanwhile, shines for JetBrains IDE users, Java/Kotlin developers, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2023, Aider describes itself as "AI pair programming in your terminal." JetBrains AI, established in 2023, positions itself as "AI assistant built into JetBrains IDEs." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Aider** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (8.0 vs 7.0), availability of a free plan. However, this does not mean JetBrains AI is a poor choice — far from it. JetBrains AI excels in its own right, particularly for JetBrains IDE users and Java/Kotlin developers. Our recommendation: if you value open-source and free, go with Aider. If deep jetbrains ide integration matters more to you, JetBrains AI is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
