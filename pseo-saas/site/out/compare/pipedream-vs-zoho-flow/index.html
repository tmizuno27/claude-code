Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Pipedream is best suited for developers, API integrations, technical teams, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Zoho Flow, meanwhile, shines for Zoho users, small businesses, budget conscious teams, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2018, Pipedream describes itself as "Connect APIs with code-level control and serverless execution." Zoho Flow, established in 1996, positions itself as "Integration platform connecting Zoho and third-party apps with automated workflows." With 22 years between them, Zoho Flow brings the maturity and proven track record of a veteran platform, while Pipedream offers the fresh perspective and modern architecture of a newer entrant.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Pipedream** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (8.0 vs 6.0), availability of a free plan. However, this does not mean Zoho Flow is a poor choice — far from it. Zoho Flow excels in its own right, particularly for Zoho users and small businesses. Our recommendation: if you value full code support with node.js and python, go with Pipedream. If excellent value within zoho ecosystem matters more to you, Zoho Flow is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
