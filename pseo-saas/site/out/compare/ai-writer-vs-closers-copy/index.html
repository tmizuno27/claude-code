Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. AI Writer is best suited for research articles, cited content, SEO writing, fact based content, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Closers Copy, meanwhile, shines for sales copy, landing pages, email sequences, conversion copywriting, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2015, AI Writer describes itself as "AI article writer that cites sources and verifies claims." Closers Copy, established in 2020, positions itself as "AI copywriting tool built on proven sales frameworks." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Closers Copy** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (6.0 vs 5.0), more features (7 vs 3). However, this does not mean AI Writer is a poor choice — far from it. AI Writer excels in its own right, particularly for research articles and cited content. Our recommendation: if you value built on proven copywriting frameworks (aida, pas, etc.), go with Closers Copy. If auto-generates citations and sources matters more to you, AI Writer is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
