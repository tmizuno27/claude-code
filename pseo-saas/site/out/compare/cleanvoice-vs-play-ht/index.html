Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Cleanvoice is best suited for podcast post production, cleaning up recorded audio, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Play.ht, meanwhile, shines for developers needing TTS API, podcast intros and voiceovers, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2021, Cleanvoice describes itself as "AI removes filler words, mouth sounds, and dead air." Play.ht, established in 2016, positions itself as "Ultra-realistic AI voice generator and text-to-speech." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Play.ht** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to more features (6 vs 4), availability of a free plan. However, this does not mean Cleanvoice is a poor choice — far from it. Cleanvoice excels in its own right, particularly for podcast post production and cleaning up recorded audio. Our recommendation: if you value high-quality voices, go with Play.ht. If excellent filler word removal matters more to you, Cleanvoice is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
