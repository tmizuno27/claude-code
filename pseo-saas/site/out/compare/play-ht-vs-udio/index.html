Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Play.ht is best suited for developers needing TTS API, podcast intros and voiceovers, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Udio, meanwhile, shines for musicians exploring AI composition, high quality AI music, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2016, Play.ht describes itself as "Ultra-realistic AI voice generator and text-to-speech." Udio, established in 2023, positions itself as "AI music creation with studio-quality output." With 7 years between them, Play.ht brings the maturity and proven track record of a veteran platform, while Udio offers the fresh perspective and modern architecture of a newer entrant.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Udio** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (8.0 vs 7.0), more competitive pricing, availability of a free plan. However, this does not mean Play.ht is a poor choice — far from it. Play.ht excels in its own right, particularly for developers needing TTS API and podcast intros and voiceovers. Our recommendation: if you value excellent audio quality, go with Udio. If high-quality voices matters more to you, Play.ht is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
