Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Automation Anywhere is best suited for large enterprises, IT operations, shared services, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Blue Prism, meanwhile, shines for regulated industries, banking, government, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2003, Automation Anywhere describes itself as "Enterprise RPA platform combining bots, AI, and process intelligence." Blue Prism, established in 2001, positions itself as "Enterprise-grade intelligent automation with digital workforce management." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict
