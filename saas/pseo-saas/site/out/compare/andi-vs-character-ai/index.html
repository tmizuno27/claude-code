Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Andi is best suited for simple search, visual answers, beginners, quick lookups, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Character.ai, meanwhile, shines for roleplay, entertainment, creative writing, character interaction, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Character.ai, established in 2022, positions itself as "Chat with AI versions of any character, real or fictional."
