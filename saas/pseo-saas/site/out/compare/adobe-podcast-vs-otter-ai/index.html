Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Adobe Podcast is best suited for cleaning up audio recordings, podcast creators in Adobe ecosystem, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Otter.ai, meanwhile, shines for meeting transcription, journalists and students, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2022, Adobe Podcast describes itself as "AI-powered audio recording and enhancement by Adobe." Otter.ai, established in 2016, positions itself as "AI meeting assistant that records and transcribes." With 6 years between them, Otter.ai brings the maturity and proven track record of a veteran platform, while Adobe Podcast offers the fresh perspective and modern architecture of a newer entrant.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Otter.ai** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (8.0 vs 7.0), availability of a free plan. However, this does not mean Adobe Podcast is a poor choice — far from it. Adobe Podcast excels in its own right, particularly for cleaning up audio recordings and podcast creators in Adobe ecosystem. Our recommendation: if you value excellent real-time transcription, go with Otter.ai. If excellent enhance speech feature matters more to you, Adobe Podcast is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
