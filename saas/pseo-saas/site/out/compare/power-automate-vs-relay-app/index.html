Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Power Automate is best suited for Microsoft 365 users, enterprises, Office heavy workflows, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Relay.app, meanwhile, shines for teams needing approval flows, operations, customer success, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2016, Power Automate describes itself as "Microsoft's cloud-based automation service for business workflows." Relay.app, established in 2021, positions itself as "Automation platform with human-in-the-loop approval workflows." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Power Automate** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to more features (10 vs 9). However, this does not mean Relay.app is a poor choice — far from it. Relay.app excels in its own right, particularly for teams needing approval flows and operations. Our recommendation: if you value deep microsoft ecosystem integration, go with Power Automate. If unique human-in-the-loop approach matters more to you, Relay.app is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
