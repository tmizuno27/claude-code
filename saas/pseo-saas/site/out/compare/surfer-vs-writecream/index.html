Who Should Choose Which?

The ideal user for each platform differs considerably. Surfer is best suited for SEO optimization, content scoring, keyword research, content strategy, making it a strong choice if you fall into any of these categories. Writecream, meanwhile, shines for cold outreach, personalized emails, LinkedIn messages, ad copy, which means it may be the better pick if your needs align with those use cases. Founded in 2017, Surfer describes itself as "Data-driven SEO content optimization and writing platform." Writecream, established in 2021, positions itself as "AI tool for personalized outreach, ads, and content." Both platforms have been in the market for a similar duration, giving each ample time to refine their offerings and build a loyal user base.

Our Verdict

After analyzing all the data, **Surfer** comes out slightly ahead in this comparison, thanks to higher user ratings (8.0 vs 5.0), more features (6 vs 5). However, this does not mean Writecream is a poor choice — far from it. Writecream excels in its own right, particularly for cold outreach and personalized emails. Our recommendation: if you value best-in-class content scoring against serp competitors, go with Surfer. If unique personalized icebreaker generation matters more to you, Writecream is the way to go. Either way, both are solid platforms that have earned their place in the market.
