Privacy Policy

SEO Inspector - Chrome Extension

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

SEO Inspector is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the extension handles and how it is used. In short: we collect almost nothing and store nothing.

Data We Collect

When you click the SEO Inspector button to analyze a webpage, the extension sends only the URL of that page to our analysis API (hosted on Cloudflare Workers). This is the sole piece of information transmitted.

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Browsing history or activity on other tabs
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • Form data, passwords, or any page content you have entered
  • IP addresses for tracking purposes
  • Device or hardware identifiers

How We Use Data

The URL you submit is used exclusively to fetch and analyze the target webpage's SEO attributes. The analysis is performed in real time and the results are returned directly to your browser.

Data Storage & Retention

We do not store any data. The URL and analysis results are processed in memory and discarded immediately after the response is sent. There is no database, no log file, and no analytics platform recording your requests.

Cookies

SEO Inspector does not use cookies of any kind - no first-party cookies, no third-party cookies, no tracking cookies.

Third-Party Services

The extension communicates with a Cloudflare Workers API endpoint for server-side analysis. Cloudflare's infrastructure may process network-level data (such as IP addresses) according to Cloudflare's own privacy policy, but we do not instruct Cloudflare to log or retain any request data on our behalf.

No other third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks are used.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. There is no data to share.

Permissions

SEO Inspector requests only the minimum browser permissions necessary to function:

  • activeTab - to read the URL of the page you choose to analyze

Children's Privacy

SEO Inspector does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly - please review this page periodically.
