Privacy Policy

SEO Inspector - Instant SEO Analysis - Chrome Extension

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

SEO Inspector analyzes the current page's SEO elements (meta tags, headings, images, links) entirely within your browser. Your privacy is important to us.

Data We Collect

This extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Zero network requests.

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Browsing history or activity on other tabs
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • Form data, passwords, or page content
  • IP addresses for tracking purposes
  • Device or hardware identifiers

Data Storage & Retention

All analysis is performed in-memory within your browser and discarded when the popup closes. No data is persisted or transmitted.

Cookies

SEO Inspector does not use cookies of any kind.

Permissions

Permissions used: activeTab (read current page's HTML to analyze SEO elements).

Third-Party Services

None - zero network requests. No analytics or tracking tools are used.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

SEO Inspector does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date.
