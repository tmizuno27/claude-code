Privacy Policy

Chrome Extensions published by miccho27 — Last updated:

Summary: Our extensions do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or any third-party data services.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all Chrome extensions published by miccho27 on the Chrome Web Store, including but not limited to the extensions listed below. When we release new extensions, they are covered by this same policy unless a separate policy is provided.

3. Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected, stored, or transmitted.
  • No browsing history, keystrokes, or form data is recorded.
  • No analytics tools, tracking pixels, or telemetry of any kind are embedded.
  • No cookies are set by any of our extensions.
  • No data is shared with or sold to third parties.

4. Local Storage

Some extensions use the chrome.storage.local API to save user preferences (e.g. cached exchange rates, tone settings, saved tab groups). This data:

  • Is stored entirely on your device.
  • Is never transmitted to any external server.
  • Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing extension data in Chrome settings.

5. Network Requests

Certain extensions (e.g. Currency Converter, WHOIS Lookup) make network requests to public APIs to retrieve non-personal data such as exchange rates or domain registration information. These requests:

  • Do not include any personal or identifying information.
  • Are made only when you actively use the feature.
  • Are sent over HTTPS.

6. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, rectify, and delete your personal data. Because our extensions do not collect or process any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. If you have questions about your data rights, please contact us.

7. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of sale. We do not collect, store, or sell any personal information. There is nothing to disclose, delete, or opt out of.

8. Children's Privacy

Our extensions are general-purpose developer and productivity tools. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Our extensions comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our extensions after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
