## Description

**Stop guessing where your money goes.**

Personal Finance Dashboard is a comprehensive Notion template that brings every aspect of your finances into one clean, visual workspace. Whether you're tracking daily expenses, managing subscriptions, paying off debt, or building savings — everything lives in one place.

### What's Inside

**Income & Expense Tracker**
Log every transaction with categories, payment methods, and notes. See daily, weekly, and monthly breakdowns at a glance with built-in filters and summaries.

**Subscription Manager with Renewal Alerts**
Never get surprised by an auto-renewal again. Track every subscription, see monthly/annual costs, and get visual alerts before renewal dates hit.

**Savings Goals with Progress Bars**
Set targets for vacation, emergency fund, a new laptop — anything. Watch your progress bars fill up as you contribute. Stay motivated with visual milestones.

**Debt Payoff Planner**
List all debts with balances, interest rates, and minimum payments. Choose snowball or avalanche strategy. See your projected payoff date and total interest saved.

**Net Worth Calculator**
Track assets (cash, investments, property) and liabilities (loans, credit cards) in one view. Watch your net worth trend over time with a monthly snapshot log.

**Monthly Budget Templates**
Pre-built budget templates with customizable categories. Compare budgeted vs. actual spending. Duplicate each month to keep a rolling history.
