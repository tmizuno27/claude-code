### What's Inside:

- **Listing Performance Dashboard** — Occupancy rates, average nightly rate, revenue per listing, and upcoming bookings at a glance. Know exactly which properties are printing money and which are sitting empty.
- **Guest Communication Templates** — Pre-written messages for every stage: booking confirmation, check-in instructions, mid-stay check-in, checkout reminder, review request, and issue resolution. Copy, personalize, send. 30 seconds per guest.
- **Cleaning Schedule Manager** — Assign cleaners per property, track completion status, link to upcoming check-ins, and log supply inventory. Never scramble for a last-minute turnover again.
- **Revenue & Expense Tracker (Per Property)** — Log every booking payout, cleaning fee, maintenance cost, supply purchase, and platform fee. Monthly P&L calculated automatically for each listing.
- **Review Response Templates** — 15 ready-to-use templates for 5-star thank-yous, 4-star acknowledgments, and negative review recovery. Professional responses in under a minute.
- **Pricing Strategy Planner** — Track seasonal rates, competitor pricing, event-based adjustments, and minimum stay rules per listing. Plan your pricing calendar months ahead.
- **Maintenance & Issue Tracker** — Log property issues, assign urgency, track resolution. Linked to expenses so you know exactly what each fix costs.
- **Supply Inventory Manager** — Track toiletries, linens, kitchen essentials per property. Set reorder thresholds and never run out mid-guest.
