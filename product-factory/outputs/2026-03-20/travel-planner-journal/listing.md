## How To Use
1. **Duplicate the template** to your Notion workspace (free Notion account works).
2. **Create a new trip** in the Trip Dashboard and fill in dates, destination, and travel companions.
3. **Build your itinerary** day by day, add packing items from preset lists, and set your budget.
4. **During your trip**, log expenses, write journal entries, and snap photos directly into the template.
5. **After your trip**, review spending summaries, complete the post-trip checklist, and mark the destination off your bucket list.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need a paid Notion plan?**
A: No. This template works perfectly on Notion's free plan.

**Q: Can I use this for multiple trips?**
A: Absolutely. Each trip is a separate entry in the database, so you can plan and archive unlimited trips.

**Q: How does the currency converter work?**
A: You enter exchange rates manually or paste them from Google. The budget tracker auto-calculates totals in your home currency using formulas.

**Q: Can I share the planner with travel companions?**
A: Yes. Share the Notion page with anyone. They can view or edit depending on the permissions you set.
