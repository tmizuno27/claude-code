## Description

### Your dream wedding deserves more than a messy spreadsheet.

You said yes — and now the real work begins. Guest lists scattered across texts and emails. Budget spiraling with no tracking. Vendor quotes buried in your inbox. Seating chart attempted on a napkin. The most joyful time of your life shouldn't feel like a second full-time job.

**Wedding Planning Hub** brings every detail of your wedding into one beautiful, organized Notion workspace.

### What's Inside:

- **Guest List Manager** — Track every guest with RSVP status, meal preferences, plus-ones, table assignments, and contact info. Filter by confirmed, pending, or declined in one click.
- **Budget Tracker** — Categorized spending (venue, catering, flowers, photography, attire, etc.) with planned vs. actual costs. See your remaining budget at a glance.
- **Vendor Comparison Board** — Compare quotes, availability, ratings, and notes for every vendor category. Never lose track of who offered what.
- **Timeline & Checklist** — Month-by-month task checklist from 12 months out to wedding day. Know exactly what to do and when.
- **Seating Chart Planner** — Assign guests to tables, track dietary needs, and flag relationships to avoid awkward seating.
- **Registry Tracker** — Monitor gift registrations, purchased items, and thank-you note status across all registries.
- **Event Schedule** — Plan your rehearsal dinner, ceremony, reception, and after-party with detailed run-of-show timelines.
- **Inspiration Board** — Save photos, color palettes, Pinterest pins, and mood board ideas linked to specific categories.
- **Dashboard** — One-glance overview: RSVP count, budget status, upcoming tasks, vendor decisions pending.
