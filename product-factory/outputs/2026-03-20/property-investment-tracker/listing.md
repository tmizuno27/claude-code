### What's Inside:

- **Property Portfolio Dashboard** — See all your properties at a glance: value, equity, occupancy rate, and monthly cash flow. One page, zero guessing.
- **Rental Income & Expense Tracker** — Log every rent payment, repair bill, insurance premium, and tax deduction. Automatic monthly/annual summaries.
- **Mortgage Calculator & Tracker** — Track loan balances, interest rates, payment schedules, and payoff timelines for every property.
- **Tenant Management System** — Lease dates, contact info, payment history, and notes for every tenant. Never lose track of who's in which unit.
- **Maintenance Request Log** — Tenants submit issues, you track status from "Reported" to "Resolved." Linked to properties and expenses automatically.
- **ROI & Cash Flow Analysis** — Built-in formulas calculate cap rate, cash-on-cash return, net operating income, and monthly cash flow per property.
- **Vacancy Tracker** — Monitor vacant units, days on market, and projected income loss. Prioritize filling the most costly vacancies first.
- **Document Vault** — Link leases, inspection reports, insurance policies, and closing documents to each property. Everything in one place.
- **Tax Deduction Categorizer** — Tag every expense by IRS category (repairs, depreciation, insurance, management fees). Tax prep in minutes, not days.

**Do I need Notion Pro?**
No! This works perfectly on the free Notion plan.

**Can I use this for Airbnb / short-term rentals?**
Yes. The income tracker supports both monthly rent and per-booking income. Just set the property type to "Short-Term Rental."

Yes. Enter your purchase price, current value, and income/expenses — the template calculates cap rate, cash-on-cash return, NOI, and monthly cash flow automatically.

Yes, future updates are included for free.
