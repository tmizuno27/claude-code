# Client Onboarding Kit

## Notion Template + 40 AI Prompts for Freelancers & Agencies

First impressions close deals. Sloppy onboarding loses clients. This kit gives freelancers, consultants, and small agencies a professional Notion-based onboarding system plus 40 AI prompts that generate every document you need — from welcome packets to SOWs to kickoff agendas.

### What's Inside

#### Notion Onboarding Dashboard
- **Client Database** — CRM with status, project type, value, start date
- **Onboarding Pipeline** — Kanban: Lead → Proposal Sent → Signed → Onboarding → Active
- **Document Hub** — All templates linked and ready to duplicate per client
- **Task Checklist** — Step-by-step onboarding tasks (auto-populated)
- **Meeting Notes Log** — Linked to each client record
